About Me

Hi! My name is Jose Lechuga and I am currently a chef who has found his passion for coding. In my work as a professional chef I utilized a lot of clunky and counter-intuitive software. Curious as to why these programs worked the way they do and if they could be improved, I started teaching myself how to code. Immediately, I knew this is what I wanted to do with my life! I love the combination of creative and logical problem-solving skills I get to use when writing code. Plus, it is an ever evolving field, so there’s always more to learn and room to innovate! As a result, I want to pursue a career as a web-developer and am taking as many online courses as I can manage, including completing Udacity's Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree. I’ve built some awesome projects, please see my portfolio of work below. I am ready to move my career to Web Development, look forward to hearing from you.
